The Thinking
Animal

How the Mind Builds a World

You believe you see the world. You do not. You see a story your brain tells about the world — built from guesses, edited for speed, and almost always good enough. This volume is about that story: how it is made, where it goes wrong, and why a three-pound organ in the dark of your skull is the strangest object in the known universe.
