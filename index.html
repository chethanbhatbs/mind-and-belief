The Reader’s Compact

One Promise, Four Colours

Every claim in this series is tagged by the kind of thing it is. Nothing supernatural, speculative, or cultural is ever smuggled in as established fact. You will always know which ground you are standing on.

Evidence-based science Debated / emerging theory Myth, religion & folklore Pseudoscience & fringe claim
What science supports

Claims backed by replicated studies, neuroimaging, controlled experiments, and clinical consensus — presented with their limits and uncertainties intact.

What cultures have believed

Demons, gods, omens, and spirits are treated as real human phenomena — powerful, meaningful, historically consequential — and explained through psychology and anthropology, never asserted as objective fact nor mocked.

The Series Architecture

Seven Volumes, Outward From the Self

The series moves in expanding rings — from the solitary thinking brain, to the feeling body, to the social crowd, to the wounded and the hidden mind, and finally to the vast structures of faith, evil, and the unexplained.
