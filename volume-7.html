The Pseudoscience Museum

Phrenology to Energy Healing

The Franklin commission of 1784 set the pattern that every careful test of every pseudo-medical theory has repeated since: when expectation is controlled for, the proposed mechanism dissolves. Mesmer’s magnetised tree, Gall and Spurzheim’s phrenology head, Hahnemann’s vanishingly dilute homeopathic pellet, Emily Rosa’s 1998 nine-year-old’s refutation of therapeutic touch — four exhibits of the same finding, with the placebo (the real, substantial mechanism) as the honest star. Ends with the ethical line: pseudoscience on placebo-responsive conditions is largely benign; on conditions where chemistry is the only thing that could save the patient, it kills.
